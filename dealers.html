Toyota booking process

Thank you for your overwhelming preference of Toyota vehicles.
While purchasing a Toyota vehicle please remain mindful of the following guidelines:

  1. Book your vehicle through Toyota authorized 3S dealership ONLY
    Do not get misled by unauthorized dealers who promise early delivery
    for a large premium amount(also known as own money)

  2. Visit the website: www.toyota-indus.com/vehicle-availability/
    to check the availability of your desired vehicle.

  3. If the delivery time on any vehicle exceeds a month, book the order
    against partial payment, from as low as Rs. 1,500,000*.
